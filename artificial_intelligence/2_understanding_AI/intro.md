# Understanding AI 

If you are here, you are here to learn. I'd like to explain AI to people in a straight forward way, hopefully. Theory of AI is complex, and you probably should come here only after you have read anything OpenAI, Anthropic, and GDM people have written. Once you've done that, I hope you can also enjoy what I have to say. I'll try to say what there is to say in a simple, straight-forward manner, but without shying away from some conceptually difficult details. We'll use as many tokens as we need. 

We're also not just going to be looking at large language models - although we'll get there. We are going to start from neural networks, then build from there. We're also going to look at things from enough detail for it to not feel too hand-wavey. 

If you are already quite knowledge on AI, you might prefer reading my 'Topics in AI' section.
